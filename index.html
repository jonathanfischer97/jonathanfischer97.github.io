About Me

I am a 27-year-old 5th year PhD candidate at Johns Hopkins University, in the lab of Margaret E. Johnson, department of biophysics. My research focuses on modeling biochemical oscillators, and I am an enthusiastic user of the Julia language.

Research Interests

My research focuses on understanding complex biological systems through computational modeling. I am particularly interested in modeling biochemical oscillators and developing novel algorithms using the Julia programming language.
